üéì CUSTOMER-SPECIFIC TRAINING STRATEGY
Option 1: Federated Learning
How: Model updates trained on customer premises, only weight gradients sent back (encrypted).

Pros: Data never leaves customer. Can aggregate learning across multiple customers.

Use: When you need to improve base model with customer patterns.
Option 2: LoRA/Adapter Fine-tuning
How: Deploy small adapter layers on customer premises. Customer trains adapters locally, you maintain base model.

Pros: Customer gets customization, your base model stays protected. Fast training.

Use: Per-customer specialization without exposing core IP.
Option 3: Knowledge Distillation
How: Customer provides labeled examples. You train a customer-specific distilled model on your infrastructure.

Pros: You control training. Customer gets optimized inference.

Use: When customer trusts you with anonymized training data.
Option 4: RAG Augmentation
How: Customer populates local vector DB with their specific knowledge. No model training needed.

Pros: Simplest. Immediate customization. Zero data exposure.

Use: Quick wins, methodology customization, team knowledge capture.

üéØ Recommended Hybrid Approach:

  • Phase 0-1: Start with Option 4 (RAG augmentation) - fastest value, zero data risk
  • Phase 2: Add Option 2 (LoRA adapters) for customers wanting deeper customization
  • Phase 3: Offer Option 1 (Federated learning) to premium customers, aggregate insights to improve base model
  • Throughout: Use differential privacy, secure enclaves, and encrypted computation where needed
  • Licensing: Charge premium for customer-specific model variants; position as competitive advantage
